About GUCC

The Georgetown University Cricket Club (GUCC) is an undergraduate organization for those who are interested in Cricket. The purpose of the club is to build a community of cricket enthusiasts who love to play, watch, or want to learn more about this amazing game. Members of the club have the opportunity to play recreationally with other students on Georgetown's Cooper Field. For those interested in more competitive formats, GUCC has competed in multiple tape ball tournaments at George Washington University. GUCC also hosts watch parties for major international and IPL matches to help foster the cricket community on campus.
